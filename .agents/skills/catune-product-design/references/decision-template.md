# Decision Template

Use this template for accepted, rejected, or proposed design-system decisions.
Keep decisions concrete and observable; avoid broad adjectives such as
"clear", "polished", or "intuitive" unless they are backed by a measurable rule.

```markdown
# Decision: {name}

Status: proposed | accepted | rejected
Date:
Owner:

## Scope

Where this applies.

## Decision

One concrete rule.

## Rationale

Why this exists for Catune users and maintainers.

## Evidence

Links to PR comments, screenshots, user feedback, files, or repeated fixes.

## Exceptions

Where this does not apply.

## Bad Example

Small example of the failure.

## Good Example

Small example of the preferred pattern.

## Enforcement

agent guidance | lint | exemplar | eval/test | coverage gap | no change

## Assumptions

What must remain true for this decision to hold.

## Open Decisions

Questions that still need human judgment.
```
