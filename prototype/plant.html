这个画面在表达什么？

What this page tells

Forest 风格的养成游戏:把抽象的"坐姿时长"翻译成一颗肉眼可见的植物。 你不需要看数字,只需要看叶子有没有蔫。

Forest-style growth game: translates abstract posture minutes into a visible plant. You don't read numbers — you just see if the leaves are wilting.

  1. 1 5 个阶段 · 种子 → 嫩芽 → 小树 → 含苞 → 结果 5 stages · Seed → Sprout → Sapling → Bud → Fruit
  2. 2 连续天数 · 7 天不弯腰 = 一朵火苗 = 进入含苞 Streak · 7 days = flame = enter Bud
  3. 3 6 个徽章 · 早晚、周末、训练、连续天数 6 badges · early/late, weekend, training, streaks

为什么这样设计？
Why this design?

- 久坐最痛苦的是"我没感觉到在变糟"——植物替你感受

- Worst pain: "I don't feel myself getting worse" — plant feels for you

- 触发即时的画面反馈,比"今日报告"有效 10 倍

- Immediate visual feedback is 10x more effective than daily reports

- 退一阶段机制防止"赢了就躺"——今天不达标就掉级

- Demote mechanism prevents "win and rest" — miss today, drop a stage
